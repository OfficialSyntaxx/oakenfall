The patron's table

Support the Hold

Oakenfall is free, runs in a single file, and makes no network requests while you play — no ads, no trackers, no accounts. If it's given you a good winter or two and you'd like to put something back, optional cosmetic packs help keep the pines standing.

These are cosmetic only — a plaque and some banner dyes. Nothing you buy changes the balance of play. Everything that matters in Oakenfall is, and will stay, free.

The packs

✦ Supporter Pack

A patron's thanks: a Patron of Oakenfall plaque in your hold, your chosen hold-name recorded on it, and premium banner dyes (royal purple, antique gold) to fly over the Town Center.

❄️ Frostmark Banners

A cold-country set of banner colours — glacier blue and frostlight — for holds that wear their winters proudly.

🔥 Emberlands Banners

A warm, volcanic set — ember red and forge orange — for the boldest holds on the map.

How it works

1 · Buy a pack

Purchase from the storefront (coming soon). You'll receive a short unlock code.

2 · Open the game

In Oakenfall, tap the ⚙ menu, then the 🎁 Redeem button.

3 · Paste your code

Your pack unlocks instantly. The code is checked on your own device — the game never goes online, so your unlocks work forever, even offline.

🏗️ The storefront isn't open just yet.
